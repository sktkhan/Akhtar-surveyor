Akhtar Surveyor — Quick Guide

1. Bearing & Distance: Do points ke E,N dalein → bearing (DMS) aur distance milega.

4. Circular Curve: Radius aur Delta angle dalein → T, L, E, M, LC milega.
6. Vertical Curve: Grade in/out aur length dalein → K-value milega.
8. Traverse: Total length aur misclosure dalein → accuracy ratio milega.
